About the Project

During our early childhood days as African children, it is not uncommon to see little kids making a sculpture of molded telephone made from clay which they often use as toys to play with and to catch fun.

There are many other beautiful crafts produced by young African Children, which goes a long way to show the level of creativity in Africa and the level of skills these ones possess.

Sadly, though, many of these skills and talents possessed by these talented minds may never come to a realization, and many pass away without having the opportunity to showcase what they have to offer to humanity and the society at large.

It is against this backdrop that the Group Managing Director of the Cubana Group of Companies, Mr. Obinna Iyiegdu, known as Obi Cubana has decided that it is now time for young tech savvies in Nigeria, Africa, be giving a chance to prove their self-worth by bringing their skills and talents to the fore. This led to the birth of the platform “Tech Ideas 4 Africa by Cubana”

Mr. Obi Cubana Founder and CEO Cubana Groups. Obi Cubana is a Nigerian serial entrepreneur and businessman who chairs the Cubana Group of companies. His career as an entrepreneur started in 2006 when he founded a night club called Ibiza Club in Abuja. In 2009. He established a hospitality club called Cubana in Owerri, Imo State. Mr. Iyiegbu is a philanthropist and serial award winner. His love for Tech and his passion to provide an enabling environment for the growth and development of the Nigerian Youths led to the formation of this noble initiative.
