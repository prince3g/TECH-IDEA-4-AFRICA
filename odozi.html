The name Odozi mobile and its meaning.

"Odozi Aku"

This is an Igbo sentence which literally means, "custodian of wealth or financial manager”

The idea of building Africa’s own Tech company provides a unique opportunity to build wealth and effectively uplift the financial position of the Nigerian youths, creating opportunities for employment and personal discovery and self-development.

The name again happens to be the social name given to the mother of our visionary boss Obi Cubana. And she is fondly called ODOZI by all. To that effect, the Tech Team is also recommending
ODOZI MOBILE as a product brand name for this Tech Initiative.
